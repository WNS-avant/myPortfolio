This system innovatively revamps the classic 'khaata' method, providing a convenient cashless experience customized for the college environment.
    The proposed system for streamlining transactions at the college canteen addresses several key issues encountered in the existing system while introducing innovative features to enhance efficiency and convenience. Let's break down the components and benefits:

    AGLET serves as the platform for managing user accounts and transactions. It utilizes the existing college identity card, transforming it into a multipurpose tool for accessing various services on campus. Through AGLET, users can access their credentials, check their syllabus, and manage their canteen deposit seamlessly. 2. Barcode Scanning Mechanism:
    Users simply scan the barcode on their college identity card to access their portal on AGLET. This eliminates the need for manual login procedures and reduces reliance on internet connectivity, as basic functionalities can be accessed offline.
    3. Minimal Internet Dependency: By utilizing the available internet connectivity on campus efficiently, AGLET minimizes the need for constant internet access. This is particularly advantageous in environments where internet connectivity may be unreliable or limited.
    4. Canteen Deposit System: The system allows users to deposit funds at the beginning of each week, ensuring they have sufficient balance for their transactions. Transactions at the canteen are expedited as users only need to scan their ID card and place their orders, with the amount deducted directly from their deposit.
    5. Streamlined Transactions: The system reduces waiting times at the canteen by eliminating the need for manual payment processing and order taking. It minimizes data consumption and eliminates the hassle of remembering passwords associated with traditional web-based systems.
    Benefits of the Proposed System: Efficient utilization of available internet resources.

    Convenient access to syllabus information.
    User-friendly barcode scanning mechanism.
    Individualized barcodes for enhanced security and tracking.
    Overall, the proposed system offers a comprehensive solution to the challenges faced by the existing system, providing a seamless and efficient experience for users accessing canteen services and other campus-related functionalities.
